# Wyckoff Spring Playbook
*Setup 2 — Bottom Fishing (Stage 1 → 2 transition)*

Load this playbook when evaluating a Wyckoff Spring / Sign of Strength setup.

---

## What is a Wyckoff Spring?

A Spring occurs in **Phase C** of Wyckoff Accumulation — a final shakeout below support that traps weak holders, then reverses sharply. It is the last "test" before Mark-Up begins.

```
Price:  -----\  /-----\  /--\    /------>  MARK-UP (Stage 2)
              \/         \/   \  / (Spring recovery)
                               \/ (Spring = shakeout below support)
Support level: ________________
```

---

## Wyckoff Accumulation Phases

| Phase | What Happens | What to Watch |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| A | Selling climax (SC), Automatic Rally (AR), Secondary Test (ST) | Volume spike on SC |
| B | Building cause — price oscillates | Volume declining overall |
| C | **Spring** — final shakeout below support | Volume on spring matters |
| D | Sign of Strength (SOS), Last Point of Support (LPS) | Price above trading range |
| E | Mark-Up begins | Confirm Stage 2 Weinstein |

---

## Spring Checklist (all must be YES before entry)

**Spring identification:**
- [ ] Price breaks below defined support (Trading Range Low) — the shakeout
- [ ] Spring volume: can be high (climax) or low (no demand selling) — either valid
- [ ] Price recovers back ABOVE support within 1-3 candles
- [ ] Recovery closes strong (near top of day's range)

**2-Week Wait Rule (MANDATORY):**
- [ ] Wait at least 2 weeks after the Spring candle
- [ ] Look for a Higher Low formation confirming Spring held
- [ ] THEN look for Sign of Strength (SOS) — expansion volume on bounce

**Sign of Strength (SOS) confirmation:**
- [ ] Volume on bounce > 80% of 20D average volume
- [ ] Price reclaims 30W (150D) Moving Average
- [ ] RS line begins turning up from bottom

**Fundamental filter:**
- [ ] CANSLIM C (current EPS) confirmed
- [ ] CANSLIM A (annual EPS trend) confirmed
- [ ] Revenue not in structural decline

---

## Entry Rules

- **Entry point**: After SOS confirmed + Higher Low formed
- **Aggressive entry**: On the Higher Low test (before SOS) — higher risk
- **Conservative entry**: On breakout above Trading Range High (Phase E entry)
- **Max position before Stage 2 confirmed**: 4% (Risk.md hard rule)

---

## Stop Loss Placement

- **Initial stop**: Below the Spring low
- **Never** below this level — if it breaks, the Spring has failed
- If stock returns to Spring low area after SOS: review and potentially exit

---

## Stage 2 Confirmation Upgrade

Once Stage 2 is confirmed (price above 30W MA, RS improving):
- Can increase position from 4% to full conviction size
- Apply VCP playbook rules for subsequent adds

---

## Invalidation Conditions

- Spring fails to recover above support within 3 candles → no Spring, avoid
- Volume on SOS < 80% of 20D avg → weak confirmation, reduce size
- RS percentile stays in bottom 30% after 4 weeks → no accumulation, exit
- Price breaks below Spring low → FAILED Spring, exit immediately
- Fundamental deterioration (C or A fails) → exit regardless of price action
